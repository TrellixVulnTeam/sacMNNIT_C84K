ABOUT HACK 36

A 36-hour tryst with gizmos, infused with youthful energy and a relentless drive for development, this is the 3rd edition of the annual Hackathon conducted by MNNIT Allahabad. Hack36 holds in store, challenges expecting to be conquered and solutions awaiting to be uncovered, for all the tech-savvy and geeks, coders and development enthusiasts. So, caffeinate, collaborate, celebrate; work, solve and work harder, reverberating the essence of the hackathon, "Every once in a while, a new technology, an old problem and a big idea turn into an innovation.
