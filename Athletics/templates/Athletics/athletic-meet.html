ABOUT ANNUAL ATHLETIC MEET

To ensure all round development,a huge emphasis is laid upon sports in MNNIT .With innumerable awards won by our college's active Athletics team, the Annual Athletics Meet is organized with great pomp and show. The 3-day event is infested with track, throwing and other field events. With the hard working members of Athletics team denied participation, the students have a plethora of fair chance to bag prizes. For other sports, Josh( intra college) and J.Lal(inter college) Memorial Tournament is the apt target.
