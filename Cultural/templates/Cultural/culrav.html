ABOUT CULRAV

The start of April will corroborate to be a week of euphoria and fresh ideation, a resplendent rostrum to hone ones flair and virtuosity and bask in the glory of triumph and merriment, known as CULRAV 2020- the annual cultural extravaganza of Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad.
From the land of “unity in diversity” , festivities and tradition , the stage is set for an all new bigger and better spree, a compelling exhibition of creativity, innovation and celebrations! Setting the national trend for a youth festival with the stronger-than-ever CULturalRAVolution, Culrav 2020 promises to culminate and cultivate the whiz bang of talent from across the country! A fete for all, inspiring unity and setting a national platform to showcase a grand colorful mixture of talent, mettle and wit, this undeniably is the place to be!
Culrav 2020 is from 8th April to 11th April, 2020. For any queries visit the website mentioned above.
